My Hobbies

In my free time, I try to keep my body and mind engaged. I like to go hiking whenever I can and I weightlift 5 days a week and am training for bodybuilding. My goal is to compete in a classic physique body building competition at some point.

I also like to draw and one day plan to buy a tattoo machine. My goal with drawing is to continue to develop my skill and maybe one day start tattooing my designs!

This year I want to get back into scuba diving as well, I got my Advanced Open Water PADI certification in 2020. That year I dove in the Georgia Aquarium and unfortunately that was the last I dove since but I plan on changing that!

When I'm not busy, I usually unwind playing some video games with my friends online, watching some tv shows, listening to podcasts, or sitting out at the beach!
